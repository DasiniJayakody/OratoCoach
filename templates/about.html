About OratoCoach

AI-Powered Presentation & Public Speaking Coach

Our Mission

OratoCoach is dedicated to helping professionals, students, and public speakers improve their presentation skills through advanced AI-powered analysis. We believe that effective communication is a key skill in today's world, and our technology makes it easier than ever to identify areas for improvement and track progress over time.

Technology

AI-Powered Analysis

Our platform uses cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning algorithms to analyze your presentation in real-time. We leverage MediaPipe, OpenCV, and custom AI models to provide accurate and detailed feedback.

Real-Time Processing

All analysis is performed in real-time, providing immediate feedback and allowing you to see your performance metrics as you present. This instant feedback helps you make adjustments on the fly.

Analysis Types

Face Analysis

Advanced eye contact tracking and facial engagement analysis. Our AI monitors your gaze patterns, head positioning, and facial expressions to provide insights into your audience engagement.

  • Eye contact quality
  • Head positioning
  • Facial engagement
Hands Analysis

Intelligent gesture recognition and classification. Our system identifies different types of hand movements and provides feedback on gesture effectiveness and variety.

  • Gesture detection
  • Movement classification
  • Gesture variety analysis
Pose Analysis

Comprehensive posture and movement tracking. Our AI analyzes your body alignment, posture quality, and movement patterns to ensure confident and authoritative presentation.

  • Posture quality
  • Body alignment
  • Movement tracking

Tips for Best Results

Environment Setup
  • Ensure good lighting for accurate face detection
  • Position yourself 2-6 feet from the camera
  • Make sure your full body is visible for pose analysis
  • Use a stable camera setup
Presentation Tips
  • Present naturally as you would to a real audience
  • Use a variety of hand gestures
  • Maintain good posture throughout
  • Practice your content beforehand
