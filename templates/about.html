About OratoCoach

Empowering confident communication through AI-powered presentation analysis

Our Mission

OratoCoach is designed to help individuals improve their presentation and public speaking skills through the power of artificial intelligence and computer vision technology.

We believe that effective communication is a learnable skill, and with the right feedback and practice, anyone can become a confident and engaging speaker. Our AI-powered analysis provides objective, detailed insights that traditional coaching methods might miss.

Advanced AI Technology

Built using MediaPipe and OpenCV, our system provides real-time analysis of facial expressions, hand gestures, and body posture with high accuracy.

Privacy & Security

All analysis is performed locally on your device. No video data is transmitted or stored externally, ensuring complete privacy and security.
